Bridging the Distance: LoRa Communication & System Integration

Published on: June 15, 2025

Connecting the Dots: Mastering Long-Range Communication

With individual components validated, the next major challenge was establishing reliable communication. From April to May 2025, we dedicated significant effort to LoRa (Long Range) communication testing and enhancements. LoRa technology is crucial for our satellite monitoring system, enabling efficient and low-power data transmission over vast distances. We pushed the boundaries, optimizing our modules for maximum range and stability.

Pushing the limits of communication! Our team performing rigorous LoRa range tests with the ground station to ensure our data can travel reliably, no matter the distance.

Watch a short clip of our LoRa communication system in action during a test run.

By the end of May 2025, we reached a pivotal moment: final testing on a breadboard with all components attached. This was our first true "system integration" test, where every sensor, communication module, and processing unit worked together in harmony. This crucial step allowed us to identify and iron out any integration issues, ensuring the design was sound before moving to a more permanent solution. Seeing everything function as a cohesive unit on the breadboard was incredibly rewarding!
